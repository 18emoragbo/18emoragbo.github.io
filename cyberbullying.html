Cyberbullying

What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is the use of technology to harass, threaten, embarrass, or target another person. The difference between bullying in the real world, and bullying in the online world is that online bullying can follow the victim wherever they go, through social media, gaming and electronic devices.

What to do if you are a victim of cyberbullying?
  • - Tell someone, and make sure that the person helps you, as the bullying could get worse
  • - Always walk away, because ignoring the bully takes away their power
  • - Resist the urge to retaliate or respond to the bully, because this may provoke them and make the situation worse
  • - Report/ block the bully, so that the bully does not have access tp talk to you
    • If you are being cyberbullied, or you know someone that is being cyberbullied, make sure to get help, before the situation escalates
