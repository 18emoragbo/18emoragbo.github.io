Downloading

What is Downloading?

Downloading is the process of copying files/ data from one computer system to another, using the internet.

Risks of Downloading

- You could download offensive/illegal files

- You could give viruses access to your computer, without knowing

- You could install data that enables crimininals to get hold of your private information

- You could download files that breach copyright

- You could download data that enables continuous popup advertisements

Advice On How To Keep Safe While Downloading Things

- Use trusted download websites, rather than random download websites

- Make sure that you have anti-virus software that is up to date

- Look at the reviews of the thing that you are downloading

- Make sure to update/rebate your device, to keep it from getting hacked easily

- If the thing that you have downloaded, has the feauture of updates, then make sure to keep to date with those updates
