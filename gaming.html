Gaming

What Is Gaming?

Gaming can be a great way for many young people, and adults to relax, chat to their friends, and have fun. These games can be played on game consoles, phones or computers. However there are some dangers, such as: you viewing inappropriate, or upsetting content (which is why there are age restrictions on certain games); some players can be abusive towards you; you may end up playing with adults that you don’t know and you could get addicted to playing games.

Five Tips For Staying Safe While Gaming

- Don’t post any personal information, like your address, email address, or mobile phone number
- Keep your privacy settings as high as possible
- Never give out your passwords
- Don’t meet up with people you have met online
- If you see something online that you don’t like, tell a trusted person, whether it is a friend or a teacher,but make sure that you tell someone
