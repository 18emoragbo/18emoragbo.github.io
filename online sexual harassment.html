What is Online Sexual Harassment?

Online Sexual Harassment is unwanted sexual aggressive pressure, on any digital platform, and it is recognised as a form of sexual violence.

How To Handle Online Sexual Harassment

Block the person from having contact with you

Report the person to the host of the software that you are using

Mind the kind of information that you post about yourself

Talk to someone that us close to you about it

Take screenshots of your chats with the person harassing you, in case they try and say that you 'asked for it'
