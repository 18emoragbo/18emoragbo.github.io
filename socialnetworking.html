Social Networking

What is Social Networking?

Social Networking is the use of different platforms, to interact with other people or for someone to find another person with similar interest to them.

Risks of Social Networking

- You could get cyberbullied

- You could share too much information about your personal life

- You are vulnerable to paedophiles

- You could share photos or videos that you may later regret

- You could be a victim of identity theft

Advice On How To Keep Safe While Social Networking

- Be yourself: Don't let people pressure you into being someone you are not

- Be nice: Treat people the way you would like to be treated, and if someon isn't being mean to you, don't retaliate, just use the 'block' too on the platform that you are using, to stop them from having access to speak to you

- Think about what you post: You could post something offensive or rude, which can get you into a lot of trouble

- Keep your personal details private: Anyone who gets hold of your personal details, for example your house adress, can try and track you down, and do all kinds of things

- Avoid meeting up with strangers: If you have to meet uo with a stranger that you have met online, make sure not to go alone meet them in a public place, tell a trusted adult where you will be going, and the time that you will be going back home
